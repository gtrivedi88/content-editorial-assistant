Help & Support

Do not enter personal information

This tool is designed for technical documentation only. Do not enter personal identifiable information (PII), customer data, credentials, or confidential business information.

Session data and privacy

Your data is not retained. User inputs and AI responses are not stored once you leave the session. Each new session starts fresh. No user content is used for AI model training.

Always review AI-generated content

AI-generated suggestions may contain inaccuracies. Verify all suggestions for accuracy and relevance before incorporating them into your documentation. The final responsibility for content accuracy lies with you.

Provide Feedback

Use the thumbs-up and thumbs-down buttons on each issue card to rate the accuracy of detected issues. Your feedback directly improves CEA.

  • Thumbs up — valid finding
  • Thumbs down — false positive

Report Issues

Found a bug or have a feature request? Open an issue on GitLab.

When reporting, include:

  • Steps to reproduce the problem
  • Expected vs. actual behavior
  • Screenshots if applicable
  • Sample content (without sensitive data) that triggered the issue

About CEA

Content Editorial Assistant is an AI-powered technical writing tool that helps writers improve the quality, readability, and consistency of their documentation.

  • 111 deterministic rules — IBM Style Guide, Red Hat Supplementary Guide, accessibility, modular docs, and minimalism
  • AI-powered analysis — LLM granular and global passes for context-dependent style checking
  • AI rewriting suggestions — context-aware rewrites
  • Supported formats — AsciiDoc, Markdown, DITA/XML, PDF, DOCX, plain text

Limitations

LimitationDetails
Contextual understanding The AI may not fully understand specialized domain terminology, product-specific context, or organizational conventions unique to your team.
Possible hallucinations Like all AI systems, CEA may occasionally generate inaccurate or misleading suggestions. Always verify AI output.
Cannot perform actions This tool provides suggestions only. It cannot directly modify your source files, publish content, or make changes outside this interface.
English only Currently optimized for English technical documentation.
No memory between sessions The tool does not remember previous sessions or learn from your past interactions.
